Back to Back Rap Concerts

A weekend of Onyx, Wu-Tang Clan, and Mobb Deep

Read the full post

This past weekend was an old school hip hop fan's dream: I got to see Onyx at a smaller venue in Innsbruck on Thursday night, then Wu-Tang Clan and Mobb Deep in Vienna the next night! So many classics performed with such raw energy, it was a thing of beauty. I think C.R.E.A.M. by Wu-Tang Clan and Slam! by Onyx were my favourite performances. I also got to meet Onyx and their whole crew by chance the day after their performance on my way to Vienna, with Fredro Starr coming over and sitting next to me for most of the ride; what a cool dude.
