## Analytics

This website uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics service. GoatCounter does not use tracking cookies and does not store personal data.
IP addresses are anonymized and not stored in identifiable form. The purpose of analytics is solely to understand general site traffic and improve content. No data is shared with third parties. You may block analytics entirely by using browser privacy settings or content blockers.

## E-mail Subscription

This site collects email addresses via ConvertKit for the purpose of sending blog updates. Emails are processed and stored by ConvertKit according to their privacy policy. You can unsubscribe at any time.
